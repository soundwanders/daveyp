My name is Davey Perron and I am a composer for music, film and video games, most known for my work in the metal band Ultra Nightmare and the synthwave band NeoNeoN.

I specialize in darker themes, running the gamut from industrial, epic and heavy to haunting, somber and melancholic. A fast-paced workflow allows me to prioritize quality above all-else, never afraid to discard ideas that don't work and iterate quickly on the ones that do.

I believe that every project has a voice, a song waiting to be uncovered — the perfect match of audio and visual — and I strive to find it.

In addition to composing, I also have experiencing with sound design, audio editing, mixing and mastering, video editing, and motion graphics.
